# Get started

[Betttr](https://betttr.xyz) is a social sports betting and prediction market mini app, built as a Frame V2 on Farcaster.

With Betttr, you can bet on sports, and soon, political and social markets, while proving your skills on a public leaderboard. Every bet is visible to the Farcaster community, and users can share their bets as Frames and earn referral fees when others copy them.

Want to follow top bettors? You can get notified whenever a specific user places a bet and instantly copy it. Because all bets and results are public, your track record speaks for itself. Build your reputation as an expert (aka tipster), gain followers, and earn referral fees as your influence grows.

**Play on [warpcast.com](https://warpcast.com/?launchFrameDomain=betttr.xyz)**
